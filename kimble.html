How to play?

Game objective:

Classic game for the whole family! Kimble is an easy and exciting game with a Pop-O-Matic die. The aim is to move all four playing pieces around the game board and into the finish area. Beware of other players: they will try to capture your pawn! If they succeed, you have to go around the board again!

Instructions:

1. If dice gives 6 user can move out from his homebase and there can be 2 moves

2. Users move clockwise

If user ends on a space with another piece on it, that game piece has to be returned to its home space

If player has all 4 pieces in his own goal space he is the winner
